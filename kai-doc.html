How opKai Works Tomorrow

  1. Copy perfusionist card into OneNote Master Op Log folder 26 (or Discord).
  2. Trigger opKai with: "opKai, write op note for [case]."
  3. Fable 5 generates the note in Khalpey voice, pulling bypass/clamp times, cardioplegia, LAA clip data.
  4. billingKai optimizes CPT/DRG codes and CDI verbiage automatically.
  5. Copy/paste into Epic or email to yourself for submission.
