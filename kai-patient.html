Sternotomy Precautions

Keep Your Move in the Tube. Picture an imaginary tube around your upper body. Keep your arms within six to ten inches when pushing, pulling, lifting, or carrying.

  • No pushing or pulling with one arm
  • No reaching overhead
  • No straining the breastbone

Robotic Surgery

No large chest incision. Small incisions between the ribs. You can move your arms freely. Lift them over your head. Stop if anything hurts.

  • Faster recovery
  • Less pain
  • No breastbone precautions
